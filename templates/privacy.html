Privacy & Data Policy

Sahaay-AI is designed with youth mental wellness and privacy as top priorities.

Data Collection

  • We only collect anonymized check-in text, mood, and language if you consent.
  • If you choose Anonymous Mode, no check-in data is stored.

Consent

On your first visit, you must choose whether to allow storage of anonymized data. Your choice is logged securely and can be changed anytime.

Right to Erase

You can delete all your stored check-ins at any time using the “Delete My Data” button in the chat interface.

Data Retention

Stored check-ins are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Crisis Handling

If you share words suggesting self-harm or danger, Sahaay-AI provides immediate helpline resources. We do not escalate to third parties without your consent.

Compliance

We align with India’s DPDP Act (2023) and global youth data safety principles. No personal identifiers are collected, and all data is stored in secure Google Firestore.
